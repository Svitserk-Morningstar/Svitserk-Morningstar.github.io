Running scripts

Fast and secure extensions.
Download Click on ME and click "Add to Chrome" on the Chrome Web Store. Violentmonkey, a safe script runner for your browser.
Dashboard Click on the Violentmonkey icon and then click on the settings icon. Taking you to your dashboard where all your scripts are located.
Adding Click on the + icon and then click on new. Copy and paste the script. Ctrl + S to save. Give yourself a high five for creating your first ever script!
