Moddiey enables you to download various types of scripts to personalize your gaming experience.

Step 1

Downloading.

Look up Tampermonkey or Violentmonkey extension in the Google web store and proceed to download it.

Step 2

Importing.

Copy the contents of the downloaded script file from the website and then paste them into a new script that you have created in Tampermonkey or Violentmonkey.

Step 3

Running.

Press "Ctrl + s" within the Tampermonkey or Violentmonkey file where you have pasted your content to save the script. After saving, reload your game to activate the script.
